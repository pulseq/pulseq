New features and performance improvements

April 2026

Please check out the current Pulseq-Matlab repository, it contains substantial performance improvements and some exciting new features. Amongst them, a substantial speed-up of the basic event library infrastructure and seq.testReport(), more consistent behavior of function that modify Pulseq objects (e.g. mr.align() or mr.scaleGrad()), allowing to detect programming errors and performance penalties. We now also introduce an initial version of the seq.autoLabel() function, which is able to add labels to a many of Cartesian sequences and thereby make them compatible with the integrated image reconstruction on the scanner.

Join us in our monthly Pulseq user group meeting

April 2026

As many of you already know, we hold our monthly Pulseq user group meetings as a monthly webinar. Unless there is a collision with international bank holidays, major conferences or alike, we meet for a hour on the first Friday of the month at 3:00 pm UTC. Anyone interested is welcome to join us!
